Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How secure is my contact data?

We use bank-level encryption and security practices to ensure your data is completely secure. Your information is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we perform regular security audits.

Can I import contacts from other services?

Yes! You can import contacts from Gmail, Outlook, CSV files, and many other popular services with just a few clicks.

Is there a mobile app available?

Absolutely. We have native apps for iOS and Android that sync seamlessly with the web application, allowing you to access your contacts on the go.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll only pay the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.
